## Overview

Discovery Education UK Help Centre. Creating and establishing a network of user-generated help sites and providing support to users with one-to-one or group tutorials, wiki documentation and written guides.

## Key Features

- **User-Generated Content**: Community-driven help articles
- **Tutorial Network**: One-to-one and group tutorial system
- **Wiki Documentation**: Comprehensive knowledge base
- **Written Guides**: Step-by-step instructional content
- **Search Functionality**: Easy access to help resources
