## Overview

Localisation of Espresso Education for Swedish publisher Liber. Working with producers and content creators to "Swedify" new and existing resources within a custom framework and on a weekly broadcast schedule.

## Key Features

- **Content Localization**: Adapting educational content for Swedish market
- **Cultural Adaptation**: "Swedifying" resources for local relevance
- **Weekly Updates**: Regular content broadcast schedule
- **Publisher Partnership**: Collaboration with Liber
- **Custom Framework**: Tailored content management system
